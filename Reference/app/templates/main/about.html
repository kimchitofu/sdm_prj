About FundBridge

FundBridge is an online fundraising platform that connects fundraisers with donors to make a positive impact.

Our Mission

To provide a transparent and efficient platform that enables individuals and organizations to raise funds for causes they care about, while making it easy for donors to find and support meaningful projects.

How It Works

  • Fund Raisers create campaigns with goals, descriptions, and images
  • Donors browse, search, and donate to campaigns they support
  • Platform Managers ensure quality and provide reporting
  • Administrators manage user accounts and system integrity
